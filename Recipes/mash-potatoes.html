Ultimate Mash Potatoes

The perfect side for almost any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 8 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cubed, cold
  • 2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • finely chopped fresh chive, for serving

Directions

  1. On cutting board, peel the potatoes. Place potatoes in a large bowl of cold water to avoid discoloration.
  2. Cut potatoes into 1-inch cubes.
  3. Add potatoes to a large pot and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 12 minutes.
  4. In a small sauce pan, bring the milk, heavy cream and garlic to a simmer over low heat.
  5. Remove the pot from the heat and strain the cream through a fine mesh sieve.
  6. Drain the potatoes in a colander and transfer back to pot.
  7. Mash the potatoes with a potatoe masher.
  8. Add cubed cold butter and salt. stir to combine.
  9. Gradually add cream mixture to potatoes, little by little, until fully incorporated.
  10. Top with freshly ground black pepper and sprinkle with chives and serve.
  11. Enjoy.
